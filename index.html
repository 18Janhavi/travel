Exploring Agra

Agra is a city in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. It is situated on the banks of the Yamuna River and is approximately 200 kilometers (124 miles) southeast of the capital city, Delhi. Agra has a rich historical and cultural heritage and is primarily known for its architectural marvels from the Mughal era.
